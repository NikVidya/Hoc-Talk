About

Hoc Talk is an ad-hoc video confrencing service with a focus on quick setup, privacy and ease of use. Other means of video conferencing necessitate lengthy, intrusive sign-up processes, downloading programs and putting up with other annoyances. With an ongoing commitment to the KISS principle, Hoc Talk seeks to break these annoying trends and offer simple, bare-bones video conferencing.

Note: JavaScript must be enabled to use this service, and you browser must support HTML5.
